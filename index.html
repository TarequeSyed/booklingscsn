Our Reading Philosophy

We value close reading over casual consumption. Sessions focus on passages rather than plot summaries. Members bring annotations, opposing interpretations are encouraged, and every reading concludes with a short written takeaway shared in the club archive.

  • Curated selections — each month features a theme chosen by voted members.
  • Notes-first — participants prepare margin notes to spark focused conversation.
  • Cross-disciplinary — we mix fiction, essays, philosophy, and science writing.
  • Open debate — ideas are critiqued respectfully and rigorously.

How sessions run

  1. Pre-read + submit two annotations.
  2. 30–40 minute focused discussion on a passage.
  3. Collective takeaway & notes published to archive.
